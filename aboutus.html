Delhi Technological University

Delhi Technological University (DTU), sometimes referred to as DelTech, formerly known as Delhi College of Engineering (DCE), is a government university located in New Delhi, India. It is one of the oldest Engineering Colleges of India & first Engineering College of Delhi. It was established in 1941 as Delhi Polytechnic, and is currently under the control of the Government of Delhi.

BAZM

BAZM

We are bAzm, a registered society in the Delhi Technological University and the main objective of our society will be to spark the light of Indian Traditional culture and patriotism in our generation by hosting various cultural programs, contests and trips.Our aim to inculcate the value of patriotism in students as they progress through the various stages in their college life and thereafter towards the nation. Our motive is to promote and sustain cultural harmony and brotherhood amongst people living in India and abroad.
